Our History

Evergreen Home Goods was founded in 2018 by Maya Brooks in Portsmouth, New Hampshire. The company began as a small local shop selling reusable bags, eco-friendly cleaning supplies, and sustainable home products.

Over the years, Evergreen Home Goods has expanded into a larger company with multiple locations. Our goal is to provide affordable products while protecting the environment. Our company supports the Sustainable Development Goals by reducing waste, using recyclable materials, and encouraging responsible consumption.

Why Use Us?

Customers choose Evergreen Home Goods because we provide high quality products while making a positive impact. Our employees care about sustainability and helping customers find products that make their everyday lives easier.

  • Eco-friendly products
  • Recyclable packaging
  • Experienced employees
  • Support for local communities
